Frequently asked questions

Everything you might want to know before (or after) you dive in.
Getting started
Is CosTrace free?

Yes — the Free plan lets you track expenses in one ledger with up to 2 members. Paid plans (Light, Regular, Gold) unlock more ledgers, members, history, and features like Funds and Payouts.

Do the people I share expenses with need to create an account?

No — you can add them by name only. They'll show up in your ledger, and if they ever create a CosTrace account with a matching invite, everything links up automatically.

What's the difference between a Ledger and a Fund?

A Ledger tracks shared expenses as you go — no starting balance, just running totals of who paid what. A Fund starts with a pool of money (or one you fill in later) and tracks how it's spent down, with each member's share calculated automatically.

Plans & billing
Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrades apply immediately; downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period so you don't lose access mid-cycle.

What happens to my ledgers if I downgrade below their limits?

You'll see exactly what needs to change (e.g. remove members, archive a ledger) before the downgrade takes effect — nothing is deleted automatically.

Is EarlyBird pricing permanent?

Yes, for the first 1,000 accounts — it's locked in for as long as your account stays active.

Do you offer refunds?

Refunds are handled by Paddle, our payment processor, in line with their policies and your local consumer protection laws. Contact us and we'll help sort it out.

Funds
What's a Purpose Fund vs a Partner Fund?

A Purpose Fund splits a pool by fixed percentages (or just records who spent what). A Partner Fund's ownership shifts automatically based on who's covered more of the spending — useful for business partners or ongoing joint investments.

Can I change how a Fund tracks spending after creating it?

Yes, anytime from Fund Settings — no need to start over.

Privacy & security
Can other members see my personal financial info outside our shared ledger?

No — you only see data within ledgers/Funds you're actually part of.

What happens if I delete my account?

It's scheduled for permanent deletion in 30 days (cancel anytime before then). Ledgers you own alone are deleted; ledgers you share stay intact for the other members.

Support
How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Use the Feedback button in the app, or email support@costrace.app.
